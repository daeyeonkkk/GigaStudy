Percussive organ emulation
--------------------------

Version 2019-07-15

Imitation of an electromechanical organ, known as a Hammond organ due to its
inventor and original manufacturer. This is a percussive sound variation.

Samples and sound bank by Strix SoundFont Team <dsoundfont123@gmail.com>, sent
to the FreePats project on June/July 2019.

Recorded from setBfree software synthesizer. setBfree is a free software
program that accurately imitates the sound of electromechanical organs:
http://setbfree.org/

This sound bank is available on the FreePats project web pages:
http://freepats.zenvoid.org/Organ/electric-organ.html
